Brain API

The most anticipated API ever

Have you ever dreamed about controlling your Brain with a dedicated and standardized API?

Brain.login(DNA.accessToken);
Brain.learn('Kung Fu');

This project intends to anticipate and document the World's first Brain API. Based on science fiction use cases and future day to day needs, we tried to explore each scenario to better understand and prevent what could append next... without fears or limits.

They already use it

//Men in Black - Agent J Neuralyzer
Brain.forget({from:'2014-01-01 00:00:01'});

//Limiless - Eddie Morra
Brain.abilities.extends({multiplier:10});

//Avatar - Miles Quaritch AMP Suit
Brain.body.move({source:AMP.accessToken});

//Superman
Brain.body.fly({to:'48.85106 2.42855'});

//Demolition man - John Spartan
Brain.dream({type:'sex', with:'Lenina'});

//Xmen - Magneto
Brain.levitate(Wolverine.Brain.body);
